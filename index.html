Rabbit's

Domestic rabbit's can be found having different colors,furs and ears. Rabbit's are a common pet who require quite a lot of care.Today we will be explaining the different breeds and adequate care rabbit's need.Rabbits can vary in size from the smaller dwarf breeds weighing less than 2.5 pounds to some of the giant breeds, weighing in at a whopping 20 pounds or more. Coats can vary in color, from whites to browns, grays, and black. Fur varies in texture and ranges from short to long.
